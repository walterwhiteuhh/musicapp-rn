### Full Description

**Klangfeld is the ultimate sanctuary for electronic music purists, DJs, and curious listeners.**

Forget opaque black-box algorithms that feed you generic hits. Klangfeld treats music discovery as an open, readable map of sonic signals, cultural lineages, and spatial textures. Designed with a sleek, minimalist cybernetic interface inspired by modular hardware synthesizers, Klangfeld lets you shape your acoustic environment with precision.

#### 🎛️ DISCOVER BY SONIC SIGNALS
Peer under the hood of every track. Klangfeld parses music into structured track features:
*   **Kick Pressure & Rhythm**: Rate the drive, physical weight, and density of the kick.
*   **Melodic & Textural Density**: Track the atmospheric layers, modular space, and harmonic richness.
*   **Energy & Drop Dynamics**: See the kinetic flow of tension, releases, and energy curves.

#### 🏷️ CURATED SCENE & STYLE WEIGHTS
Electronic music is a rich tapestry of history and sub-genres. Klangfeld uses weighted multi-tags to map out track lineages:
*   Navigate between Ambient, Minimal, Acid, Deep Tech, Leftfield, and Berlin/Detroit schools of Techno and House.
*   Understand the exact style weights and cultural contexts that make up each recommendation.
*   See editorial show and live-set references for deep, authentic context.

#### 🎚️ TAILOR YOUR TASTE PROFILE
Take absolute control. Adjust interactive horizontal sliders for track dimensions like Energy, Space, and Bass. Watch your personalized Sound Field reshape itself dynamically in real-time as you tweak your preferences.

#### 🔒 PRIVACY-FIRST, LOCAL-FIRST
Your taste is yours alone. Your taste profile is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. No corporate tracking, no forced registrations. Expand into secure AI-driven profile tag generation only when you choose.
